Who We Are

About LifeFlow Blood Bank

A blood bank is a specialized center, often within a hospital or as an independent organization, that collects, tests, processes, separates, and stores donated blood for later use in transfusions.

We operate 24/7 to ensure that life-saving blood is always available for those in critical need. Our certified medical team ensures every unit of blood is safe, tested, and ready for use.

  • ✓ 100% Safe and Tested Blood
  • ✓ 24/7 Emergency Support
  • ✓ Certified Medical Team
  • ✓ Free Blood for Patients
